Participants will learn how to navigate the Photoshop interface, understand and use various tools and techniques to edit and enhance images, and how to create and manipulate graphics. Through step-by-step instruction and hands-on practice, this course will provide a solid foundation for anyone looking to gain a basic understanding of Photoshop and its capabilities.
